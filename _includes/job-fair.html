Stockholm AI's Job Fair 2018

2018 saw the launch of our and Stockholm's first ever AI job fair and it was all that we could have asked for: it was packed with visitors and we had amazing companies showcasing their AI / ML tech and applications. From 3-people-strong companies, to big multinationals; from 1st year students, to seasoned veterans; we had a little bit of everything for everyone!
